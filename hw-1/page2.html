What jobs and projects do the work on?

Software engineers work with more than just websites, but also in other industries from automobiles and aviation to factory control and robotics; which is to say that software engineers work and help on anything new in terms of technology.

(other areas include data management, telecommunications, defense, security, computer games, business applications, operating systems, network control systems, and many others not mentioned)
