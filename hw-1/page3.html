What are the types of software engineers?

There are near to 13 different types of software engineers with each one focusing on a different priority or task.

1) Front-end: Specializes in the creation and optimization of the user interface.

2) Back-end: Specializes in software applications and logic to ensure proper data storage, logic, security, and function.

3) Full-Stack: Can specialize in either front-end, back-end, or both if it is needed to.

4) DevOps: Critical in ensuring and managing the gap between development and operations teams through effictint methods.

5) Quality Assurance: Ensuring that the software product is up to the standards of the consumer and producer, through testing.

6) Mobile App: Mainly developing mobile apps and importantly working on cross-platforms solutions for different mobile users needs.

7) Machine Learning: Specializes in designing, building, and deploying machine learning models for factories and other electronic apliances.

8) Cloud: Specializes and focus on developing cloud related programs and finding cloud-based solutions for said programs.

9) Security: Ensuring that everything is properly encoded to prevent potential data threats, breaches, and vulnerabilities.

10) Data: Pipelines and ensures the collection and analysis of data so that it is more easily understandable.

11) Embedded Systems: Specializes in designing, developing, and testing embeded systems that are integrated into the product.

12) Game Development: Write and test the code that will be used in games while working with other assets.

13) Blockchain: Focus on decentralized applications in finance, supply chain, healthcare, and others.
