What is a Software Engineer?

A software engineer is someone who helps with the designing, developing, testing, and maintaining of softwares through analysis, design, testing, and maintenance of said software.

For the task, you must ensure that your software is efficient, reliable, reusable, and properly maintained.

The most important this other than ensuring that your software functions properly, is also being able to further improve on your software's properties while also being within the possible limitation that you are given of time, budget, and requirements with the use of new tools and methods when used properly.
